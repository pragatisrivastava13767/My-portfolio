Project(1):

Pyhton Calculator

Description:

Devloped a command-line Calculator
using Python that performs a variety of mathmatical
operations. The calculator supports basic arithmetic functions
such as addition, substraction, multiplication, and division.
It also includes advanced features like power calculation,
cube calculation, radius computation, and area calculation of a reactangle based on user input.

Technologies Used: Python

Features:

Addition of two numbers
Substraction of two numbers
Multiplication of two numbers
Division of two numbers
Power calculation
Cube calculation
Radius calculation
Area or rectangle calculation
